Work Term 2

Software Developer: Camis Inc

For my second work term, I was able to secure a position as a software developer at Camis Inc.

Camis Inc is a software company based in Guelph, Ontario. They provide accomodation software to parks for things like campground reservations, backcountry camping reservations, marina reservations, equipment rentals, Point of Sale services, inventory management, and more. They also provide call center services for making park reservations over the phone.

Camis has over a dozen clients who use their software. Among these are some very large clients such as:

  • Parks Canada
  • Ontario Parks
  • Nova Scotia Provincial Parks
  • Toronto and Region Conservation Authority
  • Washington State Parks
  • Michigan State Parks

The primary Camis Inc office is located in Guelph, Ontario where there is a call centre, help desk support, developers, quality assurance, and testers, but there are several other offices in the United States that just have call centre services.
